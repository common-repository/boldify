== Description ==

Enhance your WordPress experience with our versatile plugin! With just a few clicks, you can effortlessly emphasize key words in your content. Our plugin enables you to apply bold, italic, highlighted, and underlined formatting to your text, enhancing its visual impact and making it more engaging for your readers.
